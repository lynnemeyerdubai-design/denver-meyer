The Role of Materiality in Retail Design

Material selection plays a central role in how a retail environment is experienced.

Stone, timber, metal, glass, and fabric all carry emotional and visual associations. Their textures, finishes, and proportions influence how customers interpret the brand.

High-end brands often rely on restrained material palettes with exceptional detailing. This allows the environment to feel sophisticated while maintaining focus on the product.

The goal is cohesion. Materials should support the brand narrative while contributing to a consistent spatial identity.
